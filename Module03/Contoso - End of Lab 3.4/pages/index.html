Resources for Employers

Helpful Tips on Hiring

The key to staffing your organization is knowing how many people you need. Understanding the size of your existing team can help you calculate how many additional employees are required. Another key measurement is knowing your project workload and timelines, as they greatly influence the resources required to complete the necessary tasks.

Recommended Training

Training is an important element of hiring and maintaining employees. Before hiring, have a clear understanding of a candidate's skills, their skill competency levels, and make sure to anticipate what additional training or skills may be needed.

In some cases you may be able to rely current employees to help instruct new hires with needed skills.

Things to Consider When Hiring

Follow these 3 key guidelines to ensure that you are hiring the best candidate for the role.

  1. Have a clear expectation of the job role and its responsibilies. Then make sure that the candidate's skills align with those needs.
  2. Understand the candidate's salary expectations and ensure that your organization is able to accomendate.
  3. Evaluate the candidate's understanding of the time commitment associated with the role.
